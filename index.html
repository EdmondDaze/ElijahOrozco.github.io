Intro

Hello, thank you for visiting my page. My Designs are mainly focused on mechanical and aerospace engineering blueprints however I am capable of designing most things. My main goal is to perfect my craft and work towards improving my skills. I enjoy building complex designs and engine based projects, so far, I have made a full rotary engine assembly with a V8 coming along the way. I also have knowledge in programming and coding I use C++, Java, and a little bit of HTML primarily in Object Oriented Languages and 3D environments. Thankyou for taking the time to read this.
